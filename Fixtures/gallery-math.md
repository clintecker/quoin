# Native Math

Piecewise functions, aligned derivations, and matrices — typeset natively.

$$
f(x) =
\begin{cases}
  -1, & x < 0, \\
  0, & x = 0, \\
  x^2\sin\left(\frac{1}{x}\right), & x > 0.
\end{cases}
$$

$$
\begin{aligned}
(x+y)^2 &= x^2 + 2xy + y^2 \\
x^2 - y^2 &= (x-y)(x+y)
\end{aligned}
$$

$$
\begin{pmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{pmatrix}
\qquad
\begin{bmatrix} \alpha & \beta \\ \gamma & \delta \end{bmatrix}
\qquad
\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-x^2}\,dx = \sqrt{\pi}
$$
